# Ecommerce-Store-Data-Analysis
This project analyzes the annual sales performance of Vrinda Store for the year 2022 using Microsoft Excel.  
The objective of this analysis is to identify **customer behavior**, **sales trends**, **top-performing states**, **channels**, and **categories** to help improve business decisions and increase sales.

The dashboard was built using:
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization techniques in Excel

**Project Objectives**
  The analysis answers the following business questions:
- Compare sales and orders using a single chart
- Identify the month with highest sales and orders
- Determine whether men or women purchased more in 2022
- Analyze different order statuses
- Find top 10 states contributing to sales
- Understand relationship between age and gender based on orders
- Identify the sales channel contributing maximum revenue
- Discover highest selling categories

**Key Insights**
- Women customers contributed around **65%** of total purchases
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top contributing states
- Adult age group (30–49 years) contributed nearly **50%** of orders
- Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra generated around **80%** of total sales
- March recorded the highest sales and order volume

  **Business Conclusion**
  To improve sales, Vrinda Store should:
- Focus marketing campaigns on women customers aged 30–49 years
- Target high-performing states like Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh
- Increase advertisements, offers, and coupons on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra platforms
